Business Profile

Since its founding in Harare 2015, Edmond's Tech Company, Inc. has become the leading supplier of technology based products in Harare and most parts of Zimbabwe, with several offices country wide, including the city of kings Bulawayo.

This is the only best quality, reliabe and efficient supplier of advanced technology country wide. The company looks forwad to expanding its operations to other African countries later 2019 as the business has potential for growth.
